About us

Rain Jha

Rain Jha is an IB Year 13 student at The British School in New Delhi, studying astrophysics and exploring the universe's mysteries. He has made three preliminary discoveries of near-earth objects through NASA's Citizen-Science project, IASC. In June-July 2024, he participated in the Summer Science Program (Astrophysics) at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. Additionally, he writes science fiction short stories to explore how technology impacts our world and beyond- https://www.quantumquillsproject.com/

Vedant Venkatesh

Vedant Venkatesh is a high school student at The Shri Ram School, Aravali, Gurgaon, India. Vedant has a keen interest in philosophy and history. He spent a summer at the National Museum in New Delhi and created The Museum Scout, a set of 10 activity sheets and treasure hunt for young visitors to the museum. These are available at the museum store. In June-July 2024, Vedant attended the Yale Young Global Scholars Program (Politics, Culture and Literature) at New Haven, CT. A history buff, he has designed www.thedelhimonument.org; he visited, photographed and researched over 50 monuments in Delhi. Vedant is a music student and has studied the piano for 11 years (western classical).
